What's Included

The full stack, managed

Patch & Vulnerability Management

Documented patch SLA — CRITICAL within 14 days, security-rated within 30. Firmware kept current on firewall, switches, APs. CISA KEV catalog monitored. EOL hardware flagged and replaced, never left exposed.

Threat Protection

IDS/IPS with current signatures, DNS filtering against malicious domains, endpoint protection (AV/EDR) on every device, and anti-phishing email filtering with attachment sandboxing.

Email & Domain Protection

SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configured and enforced so the church's name can't be spoofed. Microsoft 365 hardened. Domain ownership documented and brought under church control.

Network & Segmentation

Business-class firewall, VLAN segmentation (Staff · Guest · AV/Audio · IoT · Management), isolated guest Wi-Fi, and logging retained 90+ days.

Backup & Recovery

3-2-1-1-0 backup model — 3 copies, 2 media, 1 offsite, 1 immutable, 0 failed restore tests. Restores actually tested, not assumed.

Managed Services (MSP)

24/7 monitoring, remote management, patching, helpdesk, and monthly reporting. Minimally-manned by design — runs reliably with no IT staff on site.
